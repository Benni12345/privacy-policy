# Privacy Policy for Blank — New Tab

_Last updated: May 20, 2026_

Blank — New Tab (“Blank”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

Blank is designed with a privacy-first and local-first approach.

### Local Data

The extension may store the following information locally on your device:

- Notes and to-do items
- Widget configurations
- Bookmarks and shortcuts
- Themes, wallpapers, and layout preferences
- Other personalization settings

This information is stored locally in your browser storage and is not shared with us.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Blank does not:

- Collect personal information
- Sell user data
- Track browsing activity
- Collect browsing history
- Use advertising trackers
- Share data with third parties
- Require an account to function

## Permissions

Blank requires certain browser permissions in order to replace your new tab page and provide customization features. These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension.

## Data Security

Because your data is primarily stored locally on your device, you maintain control over your information. We take reasonable steps to ensure the extension operates securely and responsibly.

## Third-Party Services

Blank may optionally load external content such as wallpapers, fonts, or widgets from third-party providers. These providers may have their own privacy policies.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
